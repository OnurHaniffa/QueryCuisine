About QueryCuisine

QueryCuisine is a restaurant review and discovery platform built for food lovers. We help you find the best dining spots, read honest reviews from real diners, and share your own culinary experiences.

Discover

Browse restaurants by category, location, and price range. From street food to fine dining, find exactly what you're craving.

Review

Rate your dining experiences with 1-5 stars and leave detailed reviews to help other food lovers make informed choices.

Share

Build your favorites list, reply to reviews, and be part of a growing community of food enthusiasts across Istanbul and beyond.

QueryCuisine was built with Django as part of CSE 220 — Web Programming at Acibadem University, Spring 2026. Designed and developed by a team of four students passionate about food and technology.
